Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic interaction logs used to maintain and improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expenses, summaries, and category breakdowns within the app.
  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the product, such as hosting infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media platforms that would have access to your expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please reach out through the contact information available in the app. We will respond to all privacy-related requests within 30 days.
